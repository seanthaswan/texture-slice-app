Texture Slice

What is it?

Texture Slice is an open-source web app for extracting textures from images.

When should I reach for Texture Slice?

This is especially useful when looking to extract a texture from images of buildings or rotated objects that may need a perspective transformation to appear straightened. This technique is called homography and uses 4 points to create an area from which to calculate the matrix transforms needed for correcting the perspective.

To learn more about Homography, please see this helpful article by Socret Lee that breaks down this dense concept in a palatable format.

How do I use it?

Import an image to add it to the stage. Click and drag the 4 vertices of the slicer around to adjust the bounds to the area you wish to extract and transform.

After you've sliced a texture out, select "File > Export" to download the file.
